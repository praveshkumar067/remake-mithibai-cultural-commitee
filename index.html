FLAGSHIP ANNUAL FESTIVAL

Mithibai Kshitij:
A Euphoric Escapade

Organized exclusively by the Mithibai Cultural Committee, Kshitij is one of India's biggest inter-collegiate cultural festivals. Spanning 4 days, it brings together students from 500+ colleges across the country to compete in over 100 events, featuring massive Pro-Nites with stars from Bollywood and the music industry.
